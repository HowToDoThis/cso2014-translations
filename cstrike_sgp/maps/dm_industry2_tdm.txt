Industry2(Industry2) - Team Death Match
Plan / Created by : Nexon
Texture/Modeling/Modification : Nexon

The government has received intelligence information about the huge warehouse located on the outskirt of a city, where Terrorists had made it their main headquarters.
The Counter-Terrorist plans to stop them by an ambush but was suprised that the Terrorists are prepared for the battle. The Terrorist's scouts have exposed the ambush plan.

Goal : Kill all enemies on the map to gain team-score.
Team that score the designated kills first wins.
