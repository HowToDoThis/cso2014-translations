Hong Kong (Dazzling) – Zombie
Plan / Production : Nexon
Texture/Modeling/Modify : Nexon

In the middle of the fight between Counter-Terrorists and Terrorists, strange creatures that closely resemble zombies escaped from the ReX Research Institute and started their assault. The humans have to combine their forces to stay alive.

Human : Either stay alive or kill all zombies.
Zombie : Humans turned into zombies have to attack all humans to turn them into zombies. Zombies are stronger and faster than humans.
