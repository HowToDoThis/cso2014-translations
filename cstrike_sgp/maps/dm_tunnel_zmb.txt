Tunnel - Zombie
Plan/Level Design: Nexon
Texture/Modification : Nexon

In the middle of the fight between Counter-Terrorists and Terrorists, trange creatures appearing to be zombies escaped from the Rex Research Institute. The humans have to combine their forces to keep them alive.

Human: Either stay alive for the remaining round time or annihilate all zombies.
Zombie: Infect all humans into zombies. Zombies are stronger and faster than humans.
