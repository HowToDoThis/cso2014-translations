De_aztec

Aztec is a medium size map set in a jungle excavation site. 
Terrorists try to bomb one of the two targets while the Counter-Terrorists try to stop them.
The map is full of wide open spaces where a skilled sniper can go on a killing spree.

Terrorist Objective:
Plant the bomb at either bombsite A or bombsite B as indicated on the mini-map. Prevent the Counter-Terrorists from defusing the bomb.

Counter-Terrorists Objective:
Prevent the Terrorists from successfully detonating the bomb.
