Inferno - Gun Deathmatch
Map created by: Chris Auty (Barney) (narby@counter-strike.net)
Texture created by: Valve software

- Kill all players around.
- Kill the most of players to win.

Goal
- Victory goes to a team who kills enemy team members first with a final grenade.
- Kill every enemy on the map to gain team score.
- First to score the designated kills within the time limit wins.
