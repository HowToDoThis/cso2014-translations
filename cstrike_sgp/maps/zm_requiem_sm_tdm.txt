A long time ago, travellers remembered this place as a small and peaceful town.

Until, the town received punishment for its cruel and gloomy history...

The place became dark with a horrible smell and the town has been occupied by walking corpses.

Survivors had no choice but to abandon the town to escape.

Yesterday, beloved family, friend and colleague.

Now, monsters starved for blood and everything has changed.


"Play a requiem for the cursed spirits in this place where the 'living' things are already dead!"


Goal : - Kill any enemy on the map to gain points for your team.
       - First team to score the designated number of kills wins.
