Vertigo - Defusing a bomb
Level Design: Chris Auty (Barney) (fragged101@yahoo.com)
Texture Edit: Telnets and J0esm0e

In the middle of the fight between Counter-Terrorists and Terrorists, zombies-alike creatures have escaped from the ReX Research Institute. The humans have to join forces to stay alive.

Humans: Either stay alive till the time expired or kill all zombies.
Zombie: Turned all humans into zombies, so that your team will mutiply in quantity. The Zombies are stronger and faster than humans.
