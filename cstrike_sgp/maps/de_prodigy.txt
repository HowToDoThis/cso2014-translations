Prodigy - Plant the bomb / Defuse the bomb
Map created by: Hobbit (hobbit@nodream.net)

Counter-Terrorist: Stop Terrorist from planting C4 in either the computer lab or fuel pipe control room. Soliders are to defuse any bombs planted in target areas.

Terrorist: Ignite the C4 planted at the explosion target located in one of the bomb sites.
