Militia - Zombie
Level Design: Andrew Aumann (andrewja@home.com)

Zombies are swarming everywhere, escaping from the Rex Lab, humans will have to stick together to surpress the outbreak!

Humans: Stay alive for the duration of the round or eliminate all Zombies to win.
Zombie: Zombies are strong and faster than humans, attack all humans and try to infect the entire team to win.
