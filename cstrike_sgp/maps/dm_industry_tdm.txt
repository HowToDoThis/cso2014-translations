Industry - Team Deathmatch
Plan/Level Design: Nexon
Texture/Modeling/Modification: Nexon

The government got a secret information about a planned terror act in a factory downtown.
To keep this from happening the Counter-Terrorists enter the factory where they discover that the Terrorists are preparing in the building across the street.
The Terrorists' scouts have discovered the Counter-Terrorists as well and prepare to annihilate them.

Goal: Kill all enemies on the map to gain team score.
First to score the designated kills wins.
