Terrorist: Explode a truck or a crashed plane.
Counter-Terrorist: Prevent the Terrorists from bombing.

Background:
In a heavy snowstorm, a plane that carrying a top secret goods crashed. Inboard Aircrews escaped successfully with their parachute, and start unloading the goods to a truck. However, the heavy snowfall prevent the SWAT-team from unloading all of the goods from the plane.

Terrorist following the plane will try all means to prevent the goods to be loaded to the truck from the crashed plane or allowing the CT team to escape safely.
