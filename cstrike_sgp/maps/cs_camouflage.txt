Camouflage - Hostage Rescue
Plan/Level Design: Nexon
Texture/Modeling/Modification: Nexon

There are Terrorist forces hidden in an old building that was known to be a warehouse. As soon as this becomes known, the Terrorists kidnap four doctors from a near by goverment research facility and demand ransom in the form of an escape helicopter.
Counter-Terrorists: Kill all Terrorists that are in the way and rescue the hostages.
Terrorist: Keep the Counter-Terrorists from rescueing the hostages.

Remark: There are four hostages in this map.
