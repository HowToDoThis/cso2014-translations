Hong Kong (Dazzling) – Plant the Bomb/Defuse the Bomb
Plan / Production : Nexon
Texture/Modeling/Modify : Nexon

Suspicious Terrorists' activities have been detected in the Central Distric, Hongkong. The government has sent in Counter-Terrorists in an attempt to curb the Terrorists' activities.

Terrorist Objective: Plant the bomb at either bombsite A or bombsite B as indicated on the mini-map. Prevent the Counter-Terrorists from defusing the bomb.

Counter-Terrorist Objective: Prevent the Terrorists from successfully detonating the bomb.
