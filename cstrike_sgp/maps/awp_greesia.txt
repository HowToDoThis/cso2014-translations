Greesia Museum - Annihilation
Plan/Level Design: Nexon
Texture/Modeling/Modification: Nexon

During a fight near the Greesia Museum the Counter-Terrorist team moves into the museum to back up their snipers but happen upon the Terrorist sniper team.
Since communication with the back up squad was disrupted, both teams have to trust in only their weapons for the fight.

Goal: Get rid of all of the opposing side's snipers.
