SKYLINE - Zombie Escape
Map created by: Nexon
Texture created by: Nexon


The government had obtained intelligence, stating that zombies are appearing in development business area. 
To closely check on the situation ,they had urgently deployed the government research team.

After they have confirmed on the zombie's existence,
They would conduct an air bombing to isolate the city permanently...

Not much time are left till the air bombing!
Ride on the truck to escape towards the escape area!

Goal:
Human: Run to the escape area within the time limit given. Players must remain as a human.
Zombie: Infect/block the human players from escaping within the time limit given.
